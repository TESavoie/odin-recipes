3 Ingredient Oatmeal Banana Pancakes

A simple and wholesome Oatmeal Banana Pancake from The Lazy Dish.

My secret to using up those browning bananas.

Makes 6 small servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • Additions of your choice (vanilla, cinnamon, berries)

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet on low heat.
  2. Blend or mix bananas, eggs, and oats together.
  3. Grease skillet with prefered choice of oil or butter.
  4. Scoop about 1/6 of batter per pancake on skillet. The smaller the better.
  5. If wanting additions, add on now. Allow pancake to fully cook before flipping. They are very fragile.
  6. Flip and cook other side.
  7. Serve with favourite pancake toppings.
